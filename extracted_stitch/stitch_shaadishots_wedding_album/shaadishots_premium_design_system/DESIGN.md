# ShaadiShots Premium Design System

## Creative North Star
"Royal Wedding Keepsakes". This system treats the user interface as a premium digital wedding registry and memory chronicle. It uses elegant borders, generous whitespace, gold accents, and deep rose brand tones to convey celebratory luxury and clinical, robust functionality.

## Colors
- **Primary:** Deep Rose (`#b83280`) representing love, warmth, and brand heritage.
- **Secondary:** Warm Coral (`#ff7a59`) representing brightness and celebration.
- **Neutral:** Warm Gold (`#c58a13`) used for luxury badges, stars, and premium statuses.

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Playfair Display (Serif) to feel historic, elegant, and classic.
- **Body & Labels:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Sans-Serif) to feel fresh, modern, and highly legible.
